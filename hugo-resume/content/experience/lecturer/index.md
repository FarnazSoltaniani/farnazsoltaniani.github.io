**Lecturer and Research Assistant** at the [Center for Digital Technologies (DIGIT)](https://www.digit-research.de/), affiliated with the [Institute for Software and Systems Engineering (ISSE)](https://www.isse.tu-clausthal.de/) and the [Secure Software Engineering research group](https://www.isse.tu-clausthal.de/forschung/secure-software-engineering) at [Clausthal University of Technology](https://www.tu-clausthal.de/). My work focuses on AI‑driven solutions for secure software engineering, with an emphasis on trustworthy and privacy‑preserving machine learning.

**Research Focus:**
- Trustworthy AI and privacy-preserving AI
- **Machine learning (ML)** and **deep learning (DL)** for software engineering
- Security bug report prediction and empirical software security
- Secret detection and management in software repositories
- Evaluation of **large language models (LLMs)** for software security tasks
- Supervised by [Prof Dr. Mohammad Ghafari](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6G3BI-UAAAAJ&hl=en).


**Teaching & Academic Activities:**
- **Secure Coding** (Winter term): obfuscation, reverse engineering, malware analysis
- **Secure IT Systems** (Summer term): AI security, adversarial ML, data poisoning attack
- Supervision of M.Sc. and B.Sc. theses (≈7 students) in applied ML/DL/LLMs for security, secret detection, and adversarial ML
- Student volunteer at **FSE 2025 (Trondheim, Norway)**
