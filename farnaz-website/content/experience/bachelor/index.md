**Research Focus:**

Research focused on **Wireless Sensor Networks**: As a B.Sc. student in Information Technology at the [Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS)](https://iasbs.ac.ir/en), I conducted my thesis on the **Comprehensive Analysis of Wireless Sensor Networks** under the supervision of [Dr. Peyman Pahlevani](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Y0fAbOEAAAAJ&hl=en). My work examined network architectures, routing protocols, reliability, and performance characteristics of wireless sensor networks, with an emphasis on their applicability to real-world monitoring and communication scenarios.
